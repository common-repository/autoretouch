Easily post-process images using the AI-powered autoRetouch platform! Remove backgrounds, retouch skin, apply custom backgrounds and more!

== Description ==

Your graphics, design and marketing departments will be very happy going forward.
Finally there's an easy and fast way to edit your fashion product images, directly in Woocommerce/Wordpress.

With autoRetouch, anyone from marketing people to photo editing experts are able to simply define workflows
(the automated process sequence in which images are edited) and automatically retouch any kind of fashion product images in bulk.

In a workflow, you can define your image editing components to create an end-to-end editing sequence that can edit hundreds of apparel images at once.
Simply automate highly repetitive and non-creative steps like background removal, skin retouch, image resizing and shadows and truly upgrade your image editing operation.

Product photography processing in order to create stunning product images has never been easier since autoRetouch’s smart components are customizable and you retain full control of the final image outcome.

Get started by creating an account at www.autoretouch.com and connecting your Woocommerce/Wordpress account.

If there's any questions, check out our FAQ or reach out to us at integrations@autoretouch.com

== Installation ==

1. Unpack `autoretouch.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Open the autoretouch settings and start the account connection process.
4. Once connected you can easily submit images to autoRetouch in the image edit screens.
